About Us

Family owned and operated since 2012.

Franklinville Firearms LLC prides itself in being your "ALL-INCLUSIVE", Concealed Carry Handgun instructors.

Your understanding of how to properly handle a firearm is very important to us!

  • Breakfast and pizza lunch is included with the class, as well as snacks, water, and sodas.
  • Handgun, ammunition, targets, hearing and eye protection provided.
  • NO HIDDEN FEES! You simply show up and be ready to have fun, participate and learn.

Franklinville Firearms LLC was founded in 2012 by Christopher & Bonnie Alexander after Chris returned from service in Afghanistan. Originally, Franklinville Firearms LLC provided various trainings, focusing primarily on the North Carolina Department of Justice Concealed Carry Handgun class. Christopher and Bonnie found that following classes, students would make inquiries about buying guns and had to refer them to other businesses. They viewed this as failing to fully serve their customers & friends and applied for their Federal Firearms License (FFL).

After obtaining their FFL, Franklinville Firearms LLC ventured forward, developing a website from which they now sell firearms at very economical prices and schedule training classes. Bonnie and Chris both work full-time jobs and although most trainings take place on Saturdays and Sundays, special trainings and 1:1 training sessions are by appointment only.

Franklinville Firearms LLC now maintains a dedicated on-premises classroom and a 2 lane handgun range. The range is NOT open to the public. During the COVID-19 pandemic, classes are typically outside and amenities have been modified so as to limit exposure and germs. Franklinville Firearms LLC makes hand sanitizer, gloves and other options available for students if they wish. Firearms used in classes are routinely cleaned, sterilized and every step is taken to provide the best possible venue when it comes to training AND cleanliness.

Class Information

This section is detailed information about each class.

Concealed Carry Handgun Class

YOU WILL PAY THE DAY OF THE CLASS. $60 and 4 canned goods. Please try and bring exact change.

This is the NC Department of Justice Concealed Carry Handgun Class. This class must be a minimum of 8 hours in length, with at least 2 hours being on Legal Issues.

Students will be required to complete a 25 question exam based on the material taught in the class. Students will shoot from the 3, 5, and 7 yard lines, 10 rounds each for a total of 30 rounds.

They must get at least 25 hits within the black portion of the B-27 Human Silhouette target to qualify and we prepare you for qualification.

Franklinville Firearms LLC's CCH Class is an "All-Inclusive" class, which means we provide it all. Targets, ammunition, hearing and eye protection, use of our firearm, snacks, sodas, water, breakfast, and a pizza lunch.

On the day of the class, please dress comfortably, but appropriately. No open toed sandals or shoes. Sneakers, jeans(weather permitting, shorts are OK) and a shirt are satisfactory.

No ammo or personal handguns in the class please. Franklinville Firearms LLC provides everything that the student requires to successfully complete the class.

No hidden fees or add-ons. Upon registering, you will receive a welcome e-mail with all the specifics regarding the class. DO NOT PAY ONLINE.

Seats are limited to 12, so register fast! When contacting us, please tell us which class you are interested in.

Surviving Road Rage Class

*Although the class is free, we do ASK that students bring 3-5 cans of non-perishables that will be donated to the local food bank.

This is a completely FREE familiarization class that revolves around deadly force encounters while inside and around you motor vehicle.

Students will bring at least 150 rounds of ammunition for their respective handgun. If the student does not have their own handgun for the class, Franklinville Firearms LLC will provide a 9mm or .22 firearm FREE of charge for the training.

The student MUST still bring at least 150 rounds of 9mm or .22 ammunition.

This is not a Pass/Fail class. This is familiarization only, and students will shoot from multiple positions inside and outside of a motor vehicle.

Seats are limited to 6 students due to the large amount of range/shooting time. Couples are strongly encouraged to attend together.

Snacks, sodas, water, targets as well as hearing and eye protection are provided. Student needs to only bring the ammunition for the class.

Any questions, please contact Chris at (336)430-9332. NO PERMIT IS REQUIRED.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What does "concealed handgun" mean?

    A handgun must be covered from observation so as to prevent public view. In other words, concealed carry licensees shall not carry a handgun openly or in any other manner in which the handgun is visible to ordinary observation by a reasonable person or in public view.

  • Will there be any limitations regarding when and where a person who has the enhanced training may carry a concealed handgun?

    Yes. The new laws prohibit an individual from carrying a handgun into grievance and disciplinary meetings conducted in accordance with university procedures and lasting no more than 9 hours provided (a) the meeting is held in a room that is “no larger than necessary to complete the meeting;” (b) the individual is given at least 24 hours notice of the meeting; and (c) a notice is posted on the door in which the meeting is being held that possession of a handgun is prohibited.

  • I don't have a handgun yet, can I still take the Concealed Carry Handgun Class?

    Yes, we provide a handgun and ammunition at no extra charge.

  • Once I receive my Concealed Carry Handgun Permit (CCH) will I still have to obtain a pistol permit to purchase a handgun?

    No, once you receive your CCH permit; that will serve as your license to purchase handguns.

  • I have my CCW/CCH from another state do I still have to get a North Carolina CCH?

    If you are changing residency to North Carolina, you must obtain a North Carolina CCH Permit.

  • I’m not sure I can get a CCW permit, how can I find out?

    If your not sure you can get a conceal carry permit see the "NC Firearm laws" appendix for disqualifying offenses. You can also apply for a pistol permit at a cost of $5.00 before you start spending a lot of money on something you can’t use. You may also want to call an attorney and meet with them if your still not sure, if you have a felony conviction or any record of violence, you should contact your attorney before taking this class.
